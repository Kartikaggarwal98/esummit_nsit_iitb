Who We Are ?

NSIT IIF

The NSIT IIF is a non-profit organization run by the students of NSIT that aims at manifesting the latent entrepreneurial spirit of young students. We at NSIT IIF host various workshops, speaker sessions, innovative games and competitions for aspiring entrepreneurs all throughout the year and support them by providing necessary resources such as mentorship, consultancy, networking and angel support.

What is the objective of this event?

The key objective of this event is to provide a platform for fabulous
entrepreneurs and experts. Even if there is no financial transaction involved, we will
strive to fulfill the objective.

What's the guarantee that the panelists will financially back the ventures?

Absolutely none. This is more of a entrepreneur – expert connection event.
Panelists may choose to back none, any, or all ventures.

Who will be backing your venture

The amount of INR 10 Million need not necessarily come from a single panelist.
Multiple panelists may pool up to a total of 10 million, which is a great additional incentive for startups.
